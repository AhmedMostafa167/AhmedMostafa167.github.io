Research Contributions

Quantum Denoising in the Realm of Brain-Computer Interfaces: A Preliminary Study

Neurogenomics 2024 Conference

This research explores the application of quantum autoencoders for feature extraction from EEG data in brain-computer interfaces. We investigated whether quantum denoising could improve feature extraction from noisy signals in a cross-session experiment where participants performed tasks of varying difficulty levels. Our findings revealed limitations in the quantum approach, with no significant advantage over traditional methods, while identifying promising avenues for future quantum computing applications in neuroscience.
